Soccer

1. FC Köln (full name 1. Fußball-Club Köln 01/07 e. V., often just "FC" or "Effzeh") is a sports club in Cologne and with 138,000 members it is currently the largest club in the city.
The football club was founded on February 13, 1948. Its colors are red and white. Its mascot is a billy goat.
1. FC Köln was a founding member of the Bundesliga and remained in the highest German league for 35 years without interruption, which it belonged to after several interruptions until its seventh relegation in the 2023/24 season.

Perfume

If you didn't know: 4711 - Echt Kölnisch Wasser is a perfume. It was developed at the beginning of the 18th century and dominated the perfume market until the end of the 19th century.
The creation of this perfume goes back to the Italian perfumer Johann Maria Farina (1685-1766). In 1709 he created a perfume called Eau de Cologne from oils of lemon, orange, bergamot, mandarin, lime, cedar and grapefruit as well as herbs.

Media

Cologne is Germany's TV capital. In addition to WDR, Germany's largest public broadcaster, RTL, Germany's most successful private broadcaster, is also based here, along with other RTL Group channels such as VOX and n-tv.
The local newspapers are the Kölnische Rundschau, the Express and Duda (a newspaper for children)

Famous people who were born in Cologne:

- Agrippina the Younger (15–59) She was a great-granddaughter of Augustus. She was born on November 6th, 15 AD in Oppidum Ubiorum (now Cologne).
- Jacques Offenbach (1819–1880) German-French composer and cellist, born on June 20th, 1819.
- Konrad Adenauer (1876–1967) was the first German Chancellor (1949–1963), born on January 5th, 1876.
- Heinrich Böll (1917–1985) was a German writer, born on December 21st, 1917.

What to eat?

Classic Cologne dishes and traditional home cooking such as Halve Hahn (thick slice of medium-aged Gouda on half a rye roll with butter, pickled cucumber and mustard), Cologne caviar or Himmel un Äd mit Flönz (apples with potatoes and black pudding) and herring stew are on the permanent menu.
