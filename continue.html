Final words


Cologne is a cosmopolitan city. It is bourgeois and the people are very sociable. You will feel at home here. Cologne is easy to reach: you can come directly by plane via Cologne-Bonn Airport, or by boat across the Rhine, or by car, or, as in historical times, on foot.

Unlike in the past, the city gates are no longer locked.

I hope that this website was interesting for you and thank you for visiting it.

Mach et joot, schwenk de Hoot!
(good-bye in Cologne dialect)
